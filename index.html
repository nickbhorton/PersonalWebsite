$$\mathscr{L}=\frac{1}{2}m\left(\dot{\rho}^{2} + \rho^{2} \dot{\phi}^{2}\right) - mg\rho^{2}$$

$$\frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial \rho } = m \rho \dot{\phi}^{2} - 2mg\rho$$

$$\frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial \dot{\rho}} = m\rho$$

$$\frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial \phi} = 0$$

$$\frac{\partial \mathscr{L}}{\partial \dot{\phi}} = m\rho^{2} \dot{\phi}$$
